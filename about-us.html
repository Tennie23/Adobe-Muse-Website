About Us

Royal Gaming is an New Zealand video game and entertainment media website operated by Royal Gaming Entertainment Inc., with Teniel Nieuwoudt as founder of RG Entertainment Inc. The company is located in Tristram Street, Whitiora, Hamilton 3200 and is headed by its former editor-in-chief, Eirin Harterink. This site was created in 12 July 2015 to bring news and entertainment to the gaming community, run by Brejesh Shan.
